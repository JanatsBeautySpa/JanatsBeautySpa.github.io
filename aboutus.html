We're a small team of 5. Janat, Weenie, Feza Millicent and Maureen.



Janat is the founder and visionary of Janat's Beauty Spa. Janat is a graduate of Mount Kenya University and holds a degree in clinical medicine. Janat has a passion for fashion and seeks to share her passion with you here at Janat's Beauty Spa. With a look and style that is unique Janat is also a photo model. Janat's dream is to one day join her medical knowledge with her passion for fashion and open a medical health/beauty facility.



Winnie graduated from the NIBS school of beauty in Thika Kenya in Jul 2017 with a diploma in beauty therapy. Before joining the team at Janat's Beauty Spa Winnie worked at Fabulous Salon and N Health Beauty Spa in Thika Kenya. Weenie specializes in makeup, eyebrows, manicure, pedicure and massage. Winnie is a talented up and coming beauty therapist. To see more of Winnie's work visit her website at Makeup By Winnie.



Feza joins us from Uganda where he developed his skills and crafts. He worked at Lord's Mercy Salon before opening his own salon in Kampala, UG. Feza's specialty is nails and dreadlocks. Feza has trained many of the nail stylist working in Kenya and Uganda. Feza is passionate about his work in the beauty industry. He also is equally passionate about farming and hopes to one day have his own farm. Looking for a new style of nails or dreads then come see Feza at Janat's Beauty Spa.



Millicent graduated from Jordan College Of Technology in Feb 2018 with a degree in Cosmetology. Her basic skills are hair plaiting, massage and facial makeup. Millicent joined the team here at Janat's Beauty Spa in order to "gain work experience and develop my skills". Janat's Beauty Spa welcomes Millicent. Millicent's dream is to "One day have my own salon and spa".



Maureen is a self taught hair stylist. Maureen says "It's my god given talent". Maureen is an experienced hair stylist having worked in 2 other salons before joining Janat's Beauty Spa. She specializes in braiding, twisting, weaving and Ghanaian. Maureen choose Janat's Beauty Spa becausse "To achieve my own goal. I want to one day have my own work".


Janat's Beauty Spa prides itself on quality service at affordable prices.
