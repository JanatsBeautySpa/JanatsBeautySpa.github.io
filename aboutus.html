    We're a small team of 6. Janat, Weenie, Feza, Rose, Nur and Sylvia.



    Janat is the founder and visionary of Janat's Beauty Spa. Janat is a graduate of Mount Kenya University and holds a degree in clinical medicine. Janat has a passion for fashion and seeks to share her passion with you here at Janat's Beauty Spa. With a look and style that is unique Janat is also a photo model. Janat's dream is to one day join her medical knowledge with her passion for fashion and open a medical health/beauty facility.



    Winnie graduated from the NIBS school of beauty in Thika Kenya in Jul 2017 with a diploma in beauty therapy. Before joining the team at Janat's Beauty Spa Winnie worked at Fabulous Salon and N Health Beauty Spa in Thika Kenya. Weenie specializes in makeup, eyebrows, manicure, pedicure and massage. Winnie is a talented up and coming beauty therapist. When asked what is her dream, Winnie replies with a thoughful pause and a "Um, let me think about that". To see more of Winnie's work visit her website at Makeup By Winnie.



    Feza joins us from Uganda where he developed his skills and crafts. He worked at Lord's Mercy Salon before opening his own salon in Kampala, UG. Feza's specialty is nails and dreadlocks. Feza has trained many of the nail stylist working in Kenya and Uganda. Feza is passionate about his work in the beauty industry. He also is equally passionate about farming and dreams to one day have his own farm. Looking for a new style of nails or dreads then come see Feza at Janat's Beauty Spa.



    Rose, Nur and Sylvia are the most recent additions to our team giving us more depth and diviersity. We would like to welcome each of these talented young beauty therapist.

    Rose and Nur both specialize in weaves and braids and also perform other beauty treatments like syling, blow dry, nail polish applications, manicure and pedicure.

    Sylvia is our on site manager. Sylvia is a trained beauty therapist from Uganda and brings a passion for customer care and attention to detail to her new job.


    Janat's Beauty Spa prides itself on quality service at affordable prices.
