We're a small team of 3. Janat, Weenie and Sarah. Janat's Beauty Spa prides itself on quality service at affordable prices.


Janat is the founder and visionary of Janat's Beauty Spa. Janat is a graduate of Mount Kenya University and holds a degree in clinical medicine. Janat has a passion for fashion and seeks to share her passion with you here at Janat's Beauty Spa. Janat's dream is to one day join her medical knowledge with her passion for fashion and open a medical health/beauty facility.


Weenie graduated from the NIBS school of beauty in Thika Kenya in Jul 2017 with a diploma in beauty therapy. Before joining the team at Janat's Beauty Spa Winnie worked at Fabulous Salon and N Health Beauty Spa in Thika Kenya. Weenie specializes in makeup, eyebrows, manicure, pedicure and massage.


Sarah attended the Bukalangi Institute in Kampala, Uganda where she received her certificate in hairstyling and massage. Sarah is an experienced hair stylist having worked at several top salons in Uganda before joining the team here at Janat's Beauty Spa. Having made her mark in Uganda Sarah is excited to make a name for herself here in Kenya. Sarah dreams of becoming the best hairstylist in Africa. Her style is unique and is guaranteed to make you smile.


We also have on-call nail specialist for acrylics and stick-ons.
